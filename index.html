Amartya Basu

I am a final year MS by Research scholar advised by Dr. Ayon Chakraborty at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras, India. I am a member of the Sensing and Networked Systems Engineering (SENSE) Group at IIT Madras. My research interest lies in the broad spectrum of wireless based sensing and radio tomography, centered towards design and implementation of intelligent mobile devices that performs real-time interaction with the physical world. Prior moving to IIT Madras, I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Government College of Engineering and Leather Technology, Kolkata, India.

Radio Tomography Imaging

We design UBIQMAP, a lightweight RTI-based end-to-end system capable of mapping indoor spaces in real-time, with minimal to zero reliance over pre-deployed infrastructure.

SpecNeRF

SpecNeRF: An innovative Radio Environment Map (REM) estimation technique utilizing Neural Radiance Field (NeRF) for enhanced scalability and efficiency.

STEPMATE

A piezo electric based step counter to monitor user activity - standing, walking, running on an android application.
