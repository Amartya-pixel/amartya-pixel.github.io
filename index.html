I am a final year MS by Research scholar advised by Dr. Ayon Chakraborty at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras, India. I am a member of the Sensing and Networked Systems Engineering (SENSE) Group at IIT Madras. Prior moving to IIT Madras, I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Government College of Engineering and Leather Technology, Kolkata, India.
