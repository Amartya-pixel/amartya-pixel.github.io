I am a final year MS by Research scholar advised by Dr. Ayon Chakraborty at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras, India. I am a member of the Sensing and Networked Systems Engineering (SENSE) Group at IIT Madras. My research interest lies in the broad spectrum of wireless based sensing and radio tomography, centered towards design and implementation of intelligent mobile devices that performs real-time interaction with the physical world.
